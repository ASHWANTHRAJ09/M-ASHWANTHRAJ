Popular Cat Breeds

Maine Coon

Known for its large size and tufted ears, Maine Coons are friendly, intelligent, and great family pets.

Siamese

Siamese cats are social, vocal, and love to be the center of attention with their striking blue eyes.

Persian

Persians have long luxurious fur and a calm, gentle personality, making them perfect lap cats.

Ragdoll

Ragdolls are large, affectionate cats known for going limp when picked up and their striking blue eyes.

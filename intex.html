Popular Cat Breeds

Maine Coon

One of the largest domesticated cats, known for its tufted ears and friendly nature.

Siamese

Social and vocal cats with striking blue almond-shaped eyes and short coats.

Persian

Known for its long, luxurious fur and calm temperament, ideal lap cats.

Ragdoll

Large, affectionate cats famous for going limp when held, with piercing blue eyes.
